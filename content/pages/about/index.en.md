## What it covers

- The myblog articles follow real engineering problems: DoH resolvers, webhook deployment, Codex Desktop recovery, and Cloudflare/OpenResty paths.
- The writing keeps boundaries, tradeoffs, failure paths, and reusable notes instead of polishing every experiment into a product.

## How it is organized

- myblog owns long-form writing, feeds, and archives.
- siteforge-intro-theme owns the public entry, hand-maintained projects, service links, contact channels, and policy pages.
- The theme extends myblog with paper color, heavy pixel borders, monospace headings, and the black bear mascot.

## How it is maintained

- Project display is hand selected and does not automatically expand every repository.
- The writing page keeps the shell and layout; recent posts load in the browser from the post index.
